What We Do

From first call to keys in hand — we stay with you.

Buying in another country can feel risky. Different language. Different rules. Unknown people. Unknown paperwork. That is why our role is simple: we make the process feel clear, safe and guided.

Full Concierge Support

Private buyer consultation

A confidential first conversation to understand your goals, budget, timeline and the kind of life you want to build in Romania.

Curated property search

We match your criteria against properties we have personally reviewed, not an open database of unverified listings.

Document verification

Cadastral records, ownership history and legal status are checked before a property is ever presented to you.

Legal counsel through trusted partners

Independent Romanian lawyers, chosen for their experience with international buyers, review every transaction.

Notary coordination

We schedule and coordinate the notary process, translating and explaining each step as it happens.

Airport pickup and travel planning

Arrive without logistics to worry about. We plan your visit door to door.

Accommodation support

Recommendations and bookings for comfortable stays near the properties you are visiting.

Private property tours

Unhurried, one-on-one visits — never a group showing.

Negotiation support

We represent your interests directly with sellers, informed by local market knowledge.

Translation and paperwork assistance

Every document is explained in plain English before you sign anything.

Post-purchase relocation support

Help with utilities, registration, local contacts and the practical side of settling in.

Renovation and local partner introductions

Trusted local builders, architects and craftspeople, introduced when you are ready.

Every service is included in one guided process.

We do not publish prices for individual services here — the right level of support depends entirely on your situation. Let’s talk about what you need.
