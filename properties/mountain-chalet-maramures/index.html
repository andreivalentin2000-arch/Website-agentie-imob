A Timber Chalet Above the Valley

Vișeu Valley area · Maramureș, Maramureș County

Documents Verified

The Property

Built in traditional Maramureș style by a local carpenter, this chalet uses timber from the surrounding forest. Large windows were added facing the valley to bring in the view without changing the character of the structure. It has been used as a family holiday home and needs only light personalization to become a full-time residence.

Land Details

9,800 sqm of gently sloped forest-edge land, partly wooded, with a cleared area around the house for a garden or small orchard.
